🏈 The New Positioning — "Defense Wins Championships"

This is the strategic differentiator that makes DPI stand out from every other dental consultant. Every other consultant is coaching offense — production, case acceptance, new patients. Nobody is coaching defense.

"Offense sells tickets. Defense wins championships." — Bear Bryant

"Production is vanity. Profit is sanity."

"Your CPA does taxes. We do profit."

"Same goals. Different system. Different results."

"Trust the process." — Nick Saban, 7 championships

These lines are the consistent brand voice across every channel. They remove blame from the doctor ("you don't rise to your goals, you fall to your systems") and position DPI as the missing piece — not a criticism of what they're already doing.
